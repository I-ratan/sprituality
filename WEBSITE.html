Freedom Is Being Present

We live two lives: we live life in our thoughts and we live life as our experience of the present moment.
Freedom comes as our life in thoughts diminishes and our experience of the present moment predominates.
Freedom comes through learning how to balance thoughts and the present moment.

Humam Experience


The human experience is everything that makes us human; it is the sum of all our thoughts, emotions, and actions. It is our memories, dreams, and hopes for the future. It is the good and the bad, the happy and the sad. In short, it is everything that makes us who we are. Each one of us has our own unique story to tell. We all have different experiences that shape who we are and how we see the world. Some of us have faced complex challenges in life, while others have had more privilege and ease. But no matter our individual stories, we all share common humanity.
